I am a Professor of Mathematics and Machine Learning based in Dubai. My background is in pure mathematics, specifically [C*-algebras](https://digitalcommons.unl.edu/dissertations/AAI3465903/), and my current work focuses on the mathematical foundations and practical applications of artificial intelligence.

My research interests adapt to new theoretical and practical developments, with current projects focused on:

*   **[Explainable AI (XAI)](https://doi.org/10.56947/gjom.v22i1.4141):** Developing mathematical and algorithmic frameworks to interpret deep learning model decisions.
*   **[Imbalanced Data](https://doi.org/10.1007/s10994-022-06296-4):** Addressing the challenges of highly skewed datasets, particularly in medical diagnostics and anomaly detection.
*   **[AI in Education](https://doi.org/10.3390/su151612451):** Assessing the integration and impact of artificial intelligence tools on pedagogy and curriculum design.
