I am a Professor of Mathematics and Machine Learning based in Dubai. My background is in pure mathematics, specifically [C*-algebras](https://digitalcommons.unl.edu/dissertations/AAI3465903/), and my current work focuses on the mathematical foundations and practical applications of artificial intelligence.

My research interests are constantly evolving. I am currently focused on:

*   **Explainable AI (XAI):** I am working to develop integrated gradients-type [[1]](https://doi.org/10.56947/gjom.v22i1.4141) methods for feature attribution.
*   **Imbalanced Data:** I am interested in new ways to balance data [2](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.ins.2019.10.017) as well as studying theoretical aspects of the existing methods [3](https://doi.org/10.56947/gjom.v19i1.2639).
*   **AI in Education:** Assessing the integration and impact of artificial intelligence tools on pedagogy and curriculum design [4](https://doi.org/10.3390/su151612451).
